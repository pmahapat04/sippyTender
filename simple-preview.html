Mojito Recipe

Classic Cuban cocktail

Mojito
Cocktail ‚Ä¢ ~15% ABV

Ingredients

White rum 2 oz
Fresh mint leaves 10-12 leaves
Lime juice 1 oz
Simple syrup 1 oz
Soda water To top

Instructions

1. Muddle mint leaves with lime juice and simple syrup in a glass
2. Add rum and ice
3. Top with soda water
4. Garnish with mint sprig and lime wheel
