Mojito
Classic Cuban Cocktail

Ingredients

2 oz White Rum 10-12 Mint Leaves 1 oz Lime Juice 1 oz Simple Syrup Soda Water Ice

Instructions

  1. Muddle mint leaves with lime juice and simple syrup in a glass
  2. Add rum and ice
  3. Top with soda water
  4. Garnish with mint sprig and lime wheel
Prep Time: 3 minutes
Difficulty: Easy
Glass: Highball
